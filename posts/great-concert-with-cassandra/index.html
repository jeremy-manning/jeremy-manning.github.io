Great Concert With Cassandra

Jeremy Manning

May 9, 2025

Cassandra asked me several months ago to get her tickets to a concert for a band called San Fermin. She had been listening to their “Jackrabbit” CD (a lot) and it had become something of an obsession for her. I had only heard some of the songs from the album and they were pretty quirky (atonal instrumental stuff).

Well, we went last night. It was AMAZING I’m a huge fan now. Here’s a link to my video of the first song here. The opening band was Devine Sweater and they were outstanding (a modern version of The Sundays). San Fermin (I still don’t know how that is supposed to be pronounced) was sooo good. They have a lot of instruments and every musician is amazing. They sounded really good live and it was a complete joy. I brought ear plugs and probably should have used them (I fully expected to) but, after the first song, I had to take them out so I could, as Cassandra said earlier that day, plug into the sound completely. I’m so glad I went.

Cassandra is my favorite person to go to a concert with. She puts herself so fully into what she is experiencing that it is a joy to just be there with her. So fun.
